FlashMist Tech StartUp Stakeholder Analysis

This project analyzes the marketing performance of a retail business to optimize ad spend and improve campaign effectiveness. Using Python for data cleaning and Tableau for visualization, the analysis focuses on Click-Through Rate (CTR) and Return on Investment (ROI) across different campaign types and advertising channels. Insights reveal that Influencer Marketing (5.01% ROI) performs best, while Social Media Ads (4.99% ROI) underperform. YouTube generates the highest CTR (14.12%), making it the most engaging channel. Additionally, New York, Miami, and Chicago have the highest impressions, indicating prime locations for ad targeting. These findings help the business allocate budget strategically, enhance audience targeting, and maximize returns on marketing investments.

NovaBank Customer Segmentation Analysis

The beauty supply store’s manual record-keeping system led to inefficiencies, with employees spending 3-4 hours per week on data entry and experiencing a 10-15% error rate in inventory tracking. By transitioning to an SQLite database, sales and inventory management were automated, reducing errors by 90%, improving transaction processing by 30%, and cutting data retrieval time from minutes to seconds. Automated stock tracking also optimized inventory levels, reducing excess stock by 20% and saving 150+ hours annually in administrative tasks. This digital transformation enhances efficiency, supports scalability, and improves overall business operations.

Accredited Opioid Treatment Programs in the U.S.

I gathered and analyzed data on all opiod treatment programs enrolled with Medicare and accredited by SAMHSA (Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration) to improve access to genuine opioid abuse treatment facilities. By compiling key details—including center names, addresses, and contact information—I created a structured directory aimed at reducing the time spent searching for verified treatment centers. This initiative led to a 15% reduction in search time, enhancing efficiency for individuals seeking reliable treatment options.

Services

Brief highlights of what I can do to help your business grow.

Data Cleaning & Preparation

Ensuring high-quality data is crucial for accurate analysis and decision-making. Services include identifying and handling missing values, removing duplicates, correcting inconsistencies, and standardizing formats. Using tools such as SQL, Excel, and Python (Pandas, NumPy), raw data is transformed into a structured and reliable format, ready for meaningful insights.

Database Management & Querying

Efficient data retrieval and management are essential for streamlined operations. Services include writing optimized SQL queries for data extraction, joining tables, filtering datasets, and ensuring database performance through indexing and normalization. Proper database management enhances accessibility, security, and efficiency, enabling organizations to make data-driven decisions in real time.

Descriptive & Diagnostic Analytics

Understanding past performance and identifying key trends are fundamental to business success. Descriptive analytics provides a summary of key metrics, while diagnostic analytics uncovers underlying causes of trends and anomalies. Leveraging tools such as SQL, Python, Excel, and visualization platforms like Tableau, these insights support strategic decision-making and operational improvements.

Survey, Market Research & Business Intelligence

Data-driven market insights play a vital role in shaping business strategies. Services include survey data analysis, customer segmentation, competitive benchmarking, and trend identification. By leveraging statistical techniques and visualization tools, businesses can gain a deeper understanding of consumer behavior and industry dynamics, leading to more informed decision-making.

Financial Analysis (Sales, Supply Chain & Inventory Optimization)

Healthcare & Pharmaceutical Data Analysis
